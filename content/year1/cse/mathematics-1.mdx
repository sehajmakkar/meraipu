units:
  - title: "Unit 1: Calculus"
    content: |
      Differential calculus: limits, continuity, differentiation of algebraic,
      trigonometric, exponential and logarithmic functions. Successive differentiation.
      Leibnitz theorem. Partial differentiation. Maxima and minima of functions
      of one and two variables. Integral calculus: indefinite and definite integrals,
      integration by substitution and by parts. Applications to area and volume.
  - title: "Unit 2: Linear Algebra"
    content: |
      Matrices: types, operations, rank. Inverse of a matrix. Solution of system of
      linear equations. Eigenvalues and eigenvectors. Cayley–Hamilton theorem.
      Diagonalization of matrices. Vector spaces, linear dependence and independence,
      basis and dimension.
  - title: "Unit 3: Differential Equations"
    content: |
      First order differential equations: variable separable, homogeneous, linear.
      Second order linear differential equations with constant coefficients.
      Applications to engineering problems.
  - title: "Unit 4: Probability and Statistics"
    content: |
      Probability: sample space, events, axioms. Conditional probability, Bayes theorem.
      Random variables, expectation, variance. Discrete and continuous distributions:
      Binomial, Poisson, Normal. Correlation and regression. Curve fitting.
