units:
  - title: "Unit 1: Introduction and Supervised Learning"
    content: |
      Introduction to machine learning: types (supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement).
      Linear regression: simple and multiple. Gradient descent. Logistic regression.
      Classification metrics: accuracy, precision, recall, F1, ROC-AUC.
  - title: "Unit 2: Decision Trees and Ensemble Methods"
    content: |
      Decision trees: splitting criteria (Gini, entropy), pruning. Random forests.
      Bagging and boosting. AdaBoost, Gradient Boosting, XGBoost. Bias–variance tradeoff.
  - title: "Unit 3: Unsupervised Learning and Neural Networks"
    content: |
      Clustering: K-means, hierarchical. Dimensionality reduction: PCA, LDA.
      Introduction to neural networks: perceptron, MLP, backpropagation.
      Activation functions, loss functions, optimizers.
  - title: "Unit 4: Deep Learning and Applications"
    content: |
      CNN basics: convolution, pooling, architectures (LeNet, VGG, ResNet).
      RNN and LSTM for sequences. Introduction to transformers. Applications:
      image classification, NLP, recommendation systems. Ethics and fairness in ML.
